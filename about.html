About Me

I am currently pursuing bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering. Computer Science has been always a favourite subject to me. I started coding back in 2014 when I was in class ten. Our CPP was required in our IT course. At the very first time I did not understand anything. Later I went to home and watched a Bengali tutorial on CPP and immediately everything seems super easy. That was the first time I was introduced to coding. Before that I thought programming is very hard to learn and only meant for the super smart people. I had a used laptop which my uncle gave me. I started my first coding there. Before that I used computer only for the entertainment purposes. I immediately solved the problem that was given by the course all by myself. It seems easy and fun. Later after coming to the university, I started working with java in the university and learned my basic algorithm and data structure in java language. Today I have worked with more than 10 languages on different project.
